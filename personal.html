About

Outside of tech, I'm involved with several NYC cultural organizations including the Met Apollo Circle, The Frick Young Fellows, and New York Public Library Young Lions. I serve on the NY Junior League Research & Analytics Committee and the Sailor's Ball benefit committee.

In my free time, I enjoy learning about wine, sailing, oil painting, drawing, and singing karaoke (poorly but with great energy). I'm also an avid reader and occasionally share book recommendations through my blog, Curiosity Log.
